Ek muskaan jo dil chhoo jaaye, Ek ada jo pal bhar me deewana bana jaaye. Naaz hai us par har us dil ko, Jo sachchai aur masoomiyat ko samajh paaye. Jab wo screen par aati hai, to waqt tham sa jaata hai, Har nazar bas usi par ruk jaata hai. Na hero ki entry yaad rehti hai, Na background music ki dhun, Bas ek uski muskaan, Aur dil ka sukoon.

Wo sirf ek actress nahi, Wo ek ehsaas hai, Jo har frame me mohabbat banke chha jaata hai.
